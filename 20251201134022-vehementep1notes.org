#+title: VehementEp1Notes

* Drum Mixing Notes
**  Kick
*** EQ
+ +8-12dB at 60-70Hz (sub weight)
+ Cut 200-350Hz (Mud)
+ Boost 3-5Hz (click/attack)
+ Low-pass 10-12kHz
*** Compression
+ 4:1 ratio
+ Fast attack, fast release
+ 3-6 dB gain reduction
*** Sample Reinforcement
+ Blend:
  + low-end thump sample
  + high end click sample
** Snare
*** EQ
+ Boost 150-250Hz (body)
+ Boost 1-3kHz (attack)
+ Notch 500-800Hz (ring boxiness)
+ Boost 7-10kHz (air)
*** Compression
+ 6-10 dB GR
+ fast attack, medium-fast release
+ Ratio: 4:1 or 6:1
** Toms
*** EQ
+ Boost 60-90Hz (low) / 80-120Hz (high)
+ Cut 200-400Hz (mud)
+ Boost 3-5kHz (attack)
*** Compression
+ 4-6dB GR
+ Attack: medium-fast
+ Release: fast
** Hi-hat
*** EQ
+ High-pass 300-400Hz
+ notch painfully bright frequencies (7-10kHz)
+ lift around 3-5kHz if dull
** Cymbals
*** EQ
+ High-pass 300-400 Hz
+ Cut 2-4 kHz if harsh
*** Compression
+ 2:1 ratio
+ 1-3dB GR
** Parallel Compression (we want the drums to punch in the chest fr dawg)
*** What we doin
+ send entire kit (not cymbals) to parallel bus
+ 10-20dB GR (gain reduction)
+ blend 10-25% into the mix
