** The Three Y's
:PROPERTIES:
:ID:       2b001d85-b2d6-445f-9f09-25415f7ae8ad
:END:
~Applies to both hardware and software~
*** *Hierarchy*: Dividing a system into modules -> divide modules into something simpler
*** *Modularity*: Modules have well-defined functions and interfaces, so that they connect together easily without unanticipated side-effects
*** *Regularity*: Common modules are used many times, reducing the number of distinct modules that must be designed.
