** The Three Y's
:PROPERTIES:
:ID:       2b001d85-b2d6-445f-9f09-25415f7ae8ad
:END:
~Applies to both hardware and software~
*** *Hierarchy*: Dividing a system into  [[id:23e1e58a-a0d5-4d34-bb06-12c288d454ff][modules ]]-> divide modules into something simpler
*** *Modularity*: Modules have well-defined functions and  [[id:13bd8420-d5be-4ca1-aa73-d644c91934ad][interfaces, ]]so that they connect together easily without unanticipated side-effects.
*** *Regularity*: Common modules are used many times, reducing the number of distinct modules that must be designed.
